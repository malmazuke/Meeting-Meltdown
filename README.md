# Meeting Meltdown

Last one standing on the platforms runs the next meeting.

A silly browser game that picks who will facilitate your next guild/team meeting. Add your team’s names, hit start, and watch AI-controlled characters scramble to survive the rising lava. The last survivor is your next facilitator.

## Running the Game

**Play online:** [https://malmazuke.github.io/Meeting-Meltdown/](https://malmazuke.github.io/Meeting-Meltdown/)

To run locally, open `index.html` in your browser.

## Configuration

- **Add/remove players**: On the title screen, click **EDIT PLAYERS**. Add names one at a time or paste a list (one per line or comma-separated).
- **Share a roster**: After saving, the URL updates with `?names=...` so you can copy and share a pre-configured link.
- **Persistent storage**: Player lists are saved in your browser’s `localStorage` and remembered on the next visit.
